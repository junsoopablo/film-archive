Zoetrope

LNP-003
Category Lantern-Projectors Type Pre-cinema (Zoetrope) Manufacturer Unknown Year 1870s Country —
A 19th-century pre-cinema optical device. Sequential drawings inside a spinning cylinder, viewed through slits, create the illusion of motion. A precursor to cinema alongside the Praxinoscope.
