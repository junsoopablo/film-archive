The Dream Before Cinema — Pre-cinema

프락시노스코프 극장

LNP-001 · Emile Reynaud, 프랑스 · 1879
Invented by Emile Reynaud in 1879, this device used mirror reflections to animate sequential drawings into moving images. Predating the Lumière brothers (1895) by 16 years, it is considered the direct precursor to animated film. The most historically significant item in the collection and its chronological starting point.
